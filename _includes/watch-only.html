Watch-only

Watch only wallets allows you to keep an eye on your cold storage or paper wallet without touching your private key. Easily import your public address, xpub or ypub and watch it from your app without ever touching it.

Features

xpub/ypub/zpub

a x/y/zpub or master public key address can be passed to generate receiving addresses.

Public Address

Monitor any public address that you may have.

Receive

Generate adresses and QR codes for receiving.

Send new

Send transactions by signing them offline or with another wallet.

Animated QR codes

To facilitate the experience for big transactons.

Hardware wallets

Support for your cold storage solution and hardware devices.

How does it work

Tap on add wallet icon

Go to the option import wallet

Import with text or by QR code

Coldcard integration

Use your wallets with ease on your mobile app while leaving your keys cold. Ability to create, send and receive transactions.
